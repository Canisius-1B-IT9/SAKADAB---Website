A Brief Introduction

This website has the purpose to spread awareness and information about SAKADAB Institution, followed by providing an opportunity to those who want to donate in order to help the community with their financial needs. To keep this short, our partnered institution are experiencing financial problems which is causing educational and health related issues as a result when not addressed after a certain period of time. With this project, not only our target audience - who could be anyone interested in this will be informed about this and thus creating social awareness, but those who are willing, can donate some money to the institution through our website project. In this website, 5 web pages will be seen, all discussing about the background of SAKADAB to the proposed solution made for them, also including our SIP project. All of this will help in getting to know the institution more as well as what is being talked about in this website.
