Science Investigatory Project

The Effects of Banana Peels versus Carrot Peels on a Tomato Plant

Problem : Do banana peels work more effectively on a tomato plant compared to carrot peels?

Hypothesis : If we use banana peels on the Lycopersicum(tomato) plant, then it will grow taller.

METHODOLOGY VARIABLES

Independent Variable - The independent variable is the type of waste peels, banana and carrot peels, that will be applied to the tomato plant.

Dependent Variable/s - The dependent variable is the height of the tomato plant.

Constant or Controlled Variables - The constant or controlled variables are the size of pot, type of soil, amount of sunlight, amount of water and type of plant.

Controlled Set up

In the controlled set-up, three tomato plants will be grown however there will be no variables - banana peels or carrot peels that will be applied on the plants.

What will be used : Applicable sized pots, full sunlight exposure, 1-2 inches worth of water a week, same type of soil, same type of plant, same placement of plant

Experimental Set up 1

In the first experimental set-up (or set-up1), three tomato plants will be grown and each will be applied with banana peels as its variable/fertilizer.

Set-up 1 : The banana peel fertilizer will be applied to the tomato plants by the first day they have been planted.

What will be used : Applicable sized pots, full sunlight exposure, 1-2 inches worth of water a week, same type of soil, same type of plant, same placement of plant, same amount of fertilizer, same type of fertilizer

Experimental Set up 2

In the second experimental set-up (or set-up 2), another three tomato plants will be grown and each will be applied with carrot peels as its variable/fertilizer.

Set-up 1 : The carrot peel fertilizer will be applied to the tomato plants by the first day they have been planted.

What will be used : Applicable sized pots, full sunlight exposure, 1-2 inches worth of water a week, same type of soil, same type of plant, same placement of plant, same amount of fertilizer, same type of fertilizer
